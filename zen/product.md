- Product pitch
    - What's EmptyCup?
        - It is a simple and intuitive interior design software.
        - You plan the floorplan in 2D. 3D is automatically generated. You can customise materials and click HD renders.
        - Try out the tool and let me know what you think.

    - Customer feedback:
        - Do you have a requirement?
        - Do you know anyone who this might be useful for? How can I connect with them?

    - Why you need EmptyCup 3D...
        - It makes you more creative by getting out of your way.
        - It is pretty powerful. It can have huge impact.
        - It makes you mobile. You know how good that is.
        - It is very affordable. Like 10x cheaper.
        - You don't have to look at that monstrosity.
        - You don't have to explain your idea. That has gotta be draining...right?
        - It's cheap to try. If you like it, it's another tool in your belt. More power to you.
        - Don't worry about hiring. Avoid full time commitment. Pick a designer. There are already a few who can deliver on time. More than 1000 houses have been designed on our tool so far.
